    Exercise Wiki

    A clinical knowledge base for cardiac rehabilitation and low-impact fitness. Designed for post-MI recovery, heart-healthy exercise programming, and sustainable strength for the over-50 male.

    Parameter Target Source

    Halt CAD progression ≥ 1500 kcal/wk Secondary prevention research
    Plaque regression ≥ 2200 kcal/wk Secondary prevention research
    Phase II intensity RPE 11–13 (Borg 6–20 scale) AACVPR
    Phase II frequency 3 days/wk → 20–30 min/session AACVPR
    RT entry load 40–50% 1-RM, 2 sets × 12–15 reps ACSM Outpatient CR

    Mortality reduction 10% per 1 mL·kg⁻¹·min⁻¹ ↑ functional capacity Volume research
